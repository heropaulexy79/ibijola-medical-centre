Brief History

IBIJOLA MEDICAL CENTRE & MATERNITY HOME is a comprehensive medical outfit established purely with orthodox discipline with an avalanche of specialist units being undertaken by a myriad and cluster of professional specialists who are either qualified with their fellowship in Postgraduate medical/ Surgical disciplines or super consultants in one form of speciality or the other.

It was officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria in November 1992, though it began as a mustard seed sometime in the late 80's.
Located on the popular Lagos/Badagry Expressway, Km 31, 0f Agbara bustop, the location has been a geopgraphical advantage of being at the border-town of Lagos and Ogun state. It is officially registered with the Ogun State Ministry of Health. The neighbouring Agbara Industrial Estate gives the complex a corporate advantage.

Mission Statement

  • To provide holistic Healthcare delivery to the rural and semi-urban dwellers
  • To complement the ultra-modern medicare to urban and mega-city dwellers using the appropriate technolgy
  • To partner in public-private initiatives, employing the best of human resources to deliver accessible, affordable and acceptible quality service in line with the millenium expectation.
